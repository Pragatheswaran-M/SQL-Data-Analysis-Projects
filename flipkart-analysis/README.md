# Flipkart Customer Behavior Analysis

## Problem Statement
Analyze customer purchase behavior to identify high-value customers, top-performing product categories, and spending patterns.

## Dataset
- Orders
- Order Items
- Products
- Customers

## Key Questions Solved
- Who are the top customers by total spend?
- Which product categories generate the most revenue per customer?
- How does customer spending change over time?
- Identify customers with declining or increasing spending trends

## SQL Concepts Used
- Joins
- CTEs
- Window Functions (LAG, LEAD, DENSE_RANK)
- Aggregations

## Key Insights
- Top customers contribute a significant portion of revenue
- Certain categories dominate customer spending
- Some customers show declining order value → potential churn risk
- Spending volatility observed across multiple users

## Business Impact
- Helps identify high-value customers for targeted marketing
- Supports category-level inventory planning
- Enables early detection of churn behavior
